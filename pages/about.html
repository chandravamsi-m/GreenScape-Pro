Our Philosophy

We shape environments that inspire living.

At GreenScape Pro, we believe a garden is not just an outdoor area; it is a living, breathing extension of your home. We merge advanced biological soil sciences with high-fidelity architecture to create timeless landscapes.

Our Journey

The Evolution of GreenScape

From a single greenhouse project in Portland to architectural preservation systems.

2011

The Botanical Roots

Founded as an organic gardening crew focusing on soil conditioning. We established custom soil restoration recipes that allowed decorative plants to thrive in poor urban spaces.

2017

Landscape Architecture

Expanded into a full design firm. We integrated stone masonry, pool systems, and western red cedar carpentry to provide full turnkey estate solutions.

2026

Eco-Automation Era

Pioneering automated weather-synced drip lines, dashboard water auditing, and smart conservation techniques, leading the Pacific Northwest botanical preservation sector.

Our Pillars

The Three Pillars of Our Practice

We follow three non-negotiable guidelines to ensure that our operations are ecologically sound, aesthetically custom, and scientifically certified.

01

Ecological Integrity

All flora are native specimens selected to adapt perfectly to local rain cycles, soil types, and seasonal profiles. We use only organic composted nutrients to protect local water tables.

02

Bespoke Design

We reject templates. Your estate deserves a custom design footprint calculated based on direct sun-exposure mapping, natural slopes, and shade outlines.

03

Certified Care

Our practices are backed by certified arborist inspections. We analyze soil compaction, leaf nutrient profiles, and hydration flow diagrams to ensure root system longevity.
