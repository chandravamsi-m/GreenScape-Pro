Our Philosophy & History

Established 2011

Cultivating Spaces that Speak to the Soul

GreenScape Pro was founded with a singular focus: to change the relationship between property owners and their outdoor ecosystems. What started as a local organic garden maintenance crew has evolved into a certified design and architectural firm serving estates across Portland.

We combine local botanical expertise, organic soil mechanics, and modern smart irrigation controls to construct landscapes that thrive with minimal runoff, saving local resources while maintaining stunning visual excellence.

Our Foundational Principles

Ecological Integrity

We construct with native plants that naturally thrive in local climates, using organic soil treatments only.

Bespoke Design

No templates or generic patterns. Every property design sheet is customized for local wind, sun, and structure.

Certified Care

Every crew leader holds certified gardener credentials, ensuring precise cutting, pruning, and health monitoring.
